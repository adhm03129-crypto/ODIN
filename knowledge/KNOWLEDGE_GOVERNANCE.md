# Knowledge Governance

## Purpose

This document defines how knowledge is managed, reviewed, approved, and maintained across the ODIN platform.

The Knowledge Capability is considered an enterprise asset and must remain accurate, traceable, and auditable.

---

# Governance Principles

Knowledge shall be:

- Accurate
- Verified
- Version Controlled
- Reviewable
- Traceable
- Consistent

---

# Roles

## Knowledge Author

Creates new knowledge.

## Reviewer

Validates correctness.

## Approver

Approves publication.

## Consumer

Uses official knowledge without modification.

---

# Change Process

Knowledge follows this lifecycle:

Draft

↓

Review

↓

Approved

↓

Published

↓

Deprecated

---

# Review Policy

Knowledge should be reviewed:

- After game updates
- Before major releases
- When inconsistencies are discovered
