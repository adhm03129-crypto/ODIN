# ODIN

**ODIN – Enterprise Gaming Intelligence Platform**

> Building intelligent, explainable, and scalable gaming intelligence systems.

---

## Overview

ODIN is an enterprise-grade software platform designed to build AI-powered gaming intelligence solutions.

The platform provides a reusable engineering foundation capable of supporting multiple games through modular adapters while sharing common services, infrastructure, and knowledge.

The first supported product is:

> **Whiteout Survival Intelligence Platform (WSIP)**

---

## Vision

Create the world's most trusted gaming intelligence platform by combining verified knowledge, artificial intelligence, OCR, analytics, and planning into a unified ecosystem.
